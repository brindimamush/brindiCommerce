CommerceHub is a **self-hosted, single-business e-commerce software platform** that enables small businesses to manage their entire online sales operation from one centralized backend. Each installation is dedicated to a single business—such as a clothing store, electronics shop, pharmacy, restaurant, bakery, or grocery—and is fully configurable to support different business types without changing the core software.

The platform follows an **API-first architecture**, where a single backend powers multiple customer interfaces, including a **web storefront, Telegram bot, and mobile application**, while providing an administrative web dashboard for business management. It includes modules for product catalog management, inventory, orders, customers, payments, shipping, notifications, analytics, and promotions, ensuring that all interfaces share the same data and business logic.

CommerceHub is designed as a **production-ready, modular monolith** that is secure, scalable, maintainable, and extensible. It is intended to serve as an all-in-one commerce solution that can start on a small VPS for a single business and grow over time with advanced capabilities such as AI features, POS integration, multi-warehouse support, CRM/ERP integrations, and a plugin ecosystem.
